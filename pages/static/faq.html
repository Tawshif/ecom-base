Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and returns.

Orders & Shipping

How long will it take to receive my order?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. We also offer expedited 2-day shipping at checkout. Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same day.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to the US, Canada, UK, and most of Europe. International shipping usually takes 7-14 business days. Customs fees may apply depending on your location.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order directly from your Account Dashboard.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day risk-free return policy. If you aren't completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Please ensure items are in their original packaging.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the order you wish to return, and click "Initiate Return." You will receive a prepaid shipping label via email.
