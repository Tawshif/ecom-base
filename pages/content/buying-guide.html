Buying Guide

Which Mouse is Right for You?

From coding and design to gaming and travel, find the perfect mouse to match your workflow.

Choosing a mouse is a highly personal decision. Your grip style, hand size, and daily tasks all dictate which form factor and feature set will serve you best. Use this guide to navigate our lineup and find your perfect match.

1. The Power User: Master Series

If you spend your days analyzing spreadsheets, writing code, or editing video, you need a mouse that can keep up. The MX Master 3S is designed specifically for power users.

  • Key Features: MagSpeed electromagnetic scrolling, dual scroll wheels, app-specific customizations, and silent clicks.
  • Best For: Software developers, video editors, and data analysts.
  • Grip Style: Palm grip (ideal for medium to large hands).

2. The Digital Nomad: Anywhere Series

Work happens everywhere—from coffee shops to airport lounges. The MX Anywhere 3S gives you flagship performance in a compact, travel-friendly chassis.

  • Key Features: Tracks on any surface (including glass), compact size, silicone side grips, USB-C fast charging.
  • Best For: Remote workers, travelers, and students.
  • Grip Style: Fingertip or claw grip (great for smaller hands).

3. The Ergonomic Purist: Ergo & Vertical

If you experience wrist pain or simply want to proactively protect your joints, our ergonomic line is the answer. The MX Vertical places your hand at a natural 57-degree angle, while the MX Ergo trackball means you never have to move your wrist at all.

"Switching to a vertical mouse reduced my wrist fatigue by 80% within the first week." – Alex, Software Engineer
